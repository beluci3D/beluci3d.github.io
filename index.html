// about me

HEY, I'M
BELUCI

I'm a passionate Maker — someone who doesn't just dream up ideas, but actually builds them. With my 3D printer I create objects ranging from purely decorative pieces to fully functional tools and enclosures.

My love for electronics drives me to solder circuits, program microcontrollers and build devices that actually do something useful — or just look really cool.

On my YouTube channel I share electronics projects — honest, transparent content from first idea to final test. On MakerWorld I upload my best 3D designs so you can print them yourself.
